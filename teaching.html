Multi-disciplinary Evidence-Based Teaching Approach

    Like my approach to research, my teaching emphasizes a systems approach to transportation and a multidisciplinary approach to engineering as a whole. In my teaching I strive to strike a balance between providing students with the theoretical and conceptual knowledge necessary to understand transportation systems and making sure they develop the analytical skills they need to tackle complex transportation engineering problems.
