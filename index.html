About Me

I am a PhD Candidate at the Northwestern University Transportation Center in the Transportation Systems Analysis and Planning program at the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering. I am currently working on my dissertation supervised by Dr. Hani S. Mahmassani.

Research Highlights

  • IEEE ITS Conference 2021 Workshop Chair

    I am co-organizing and chairing a special workshop titled "Next Generation Transportation Networks: Emerging Technologies, Data Analytics and Perspectives" during the 2021 International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC). Workshop website coming soon!

  • Traffic Congestion Prediction

    Read my recent journal article titled
    "Traffic flow breakdown prediction using machine learning approaches" recently published in the Transportation Research Record.

  • GIS Platform for Travel Time Reliability Estimation

    I recently developed an ArcGIS Pro tool for the estimation of path travel time distributions and performing path finding in stochastic dynamic transportation networks.

Teaching Highlights

  • Teaching-As-Research Project

    In Spring 2020 I co-taught a course on "Data Analytics for Transportation and Urban Infrastructure Applications". I implemented social pedagogies focused on content relevance through real-world applications to foster student motivation and build a learning community in the remote setting.
