Welcome

Some Recent Projects

IEEE ITS Conference 2020 Best Presentation

My presentation at the IEEE ITS Conference titled "Reliable least-time path estimation and computation in stochastic time-varying networks with spatio-temporal dependencies" received the 3rd prize for the best presentation award.

My Most Recent Publication

The article titled "Traffic flow breakdown prediction using machine learning approaches" was recently published in the Transportation Research Record.

A GIS Platform for Estimation of Travel Time Distributions

I recently developed an ArcGIS Pro toolbox for the estimation of path travel time distributions and performing path finding under uncertainty.

Teaching-As-Research Project

I implemented social pedagogies focused on content relevance via applications in order to foster student motivation and building a learning community in a blended remote learning setting.
